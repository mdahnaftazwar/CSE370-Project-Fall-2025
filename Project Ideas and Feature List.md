# CSE370-Project-Fall-2025
CSE370 [Database Systems] Final Project for the Semester, Fall 2025. BRAC University.

Project Idea 1: Cattle Farm Management System.
1. Individual animal biometric and health tracking. [Unique Feature: Implement a Predictive Health Score based on historical treatment frequency and age]
2. Feed and inventory management. [Unique Feature: Track inventory of different types, supplements and medication. [Unique Feature: FIFO system for inventory valuation and generate early warnings when feed is approaching its expiry date, minimizing waste and proper cost accounting.]
3. Yield and production tracking.
4. Breeding status and log of the cattles.
5. Seperate status tracking and valuation for cows to be sold during religious festivals.
6. Health and Treatment event log.
7. Assignment of staffs to individual cattles
8. Staff details and salary table.

Project Idea 2: Ecommerce Clothes Shopping Platform
1. Product Table
2. Variant table linked to Product (One product has many variants)
3. Order Table
4. Checkout table and product inventory management only after the payment transaction is successful.
5. Coupon Code validation.
6. Order history Retrieval and aggregation.
